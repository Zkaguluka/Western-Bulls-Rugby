THE BULLS COMMUNITY: BULLS COMMUNITY PROGRAMS

The Western Bulls have a tradition of supporting charities and community groups across the county, providing support and fundraising opportunities wherever possible.

Charity and community support occurs in a number of ways:

  • With our official matchday charity partners, selected on a bi-annual basis and with a focus on matchday activations.
  • By supporting community rugby programs and clubs across the state, in conjunction with the Kenya Rugby Union.

All these avenues of support include the donation of resources and time (appearances by coaches, players and our mascot) as well as through monetary-value items including memorabilia.

Western Bulls Rugby Club also receives a large number of additional requests from charities and from the rugby community. We strive to accommodate as many requests as possible, however, please note that requests received are not always able to be fulfilled. Please visit our charity requests page for more information on eligibility and how to make a request.
Read more about theWestern Bulls Charity Partnerships through formal charity partners selected for their contribution to the lives of local and county-wide communities, with a specific focus on health, wellbeing and young people

During the 2014 and 2015 seasons the Western Bulls contributed more than 1,000 hours to appearances by the rugby team. The personal commitment of our Bulls players to supporting those less fortunate also sees many of the players support other charities and their own individual causes. Feel free to contact us for more information.
